Task Brief
Available when contest starts

Simba Supermarket: Platform Rebuild Contest. Rebuild Simba as a modern, fast, mobile-first supermarket platform for Rwanda. This is a fresh final-set contest: submit a new GitHub repository and a new live demo for this version.

Requirements

✓ Display all 789 products from the dataset grouped by the 11 categories, with subcategory filtering.
✓ Full-text search with instant results. Include filters for category, price range in RWF, and availability; include sorting by price, name, and relevance.
✓ Cart: add items, remove items, update quantity, show a running RWF total, persist across reloads, and show an item-count badge.
✓ Responsive mobile-first design from 360px upward, with touch-friendly targets of at least 44px and clear mobile navigation.
✓ User authentication: register with name, email, Rwandan phone, password, and Google option; login; persistent session; profile page.
✓ Delivery management: saved addresses, Kigali district zones, delivery fee by zone, and preferred delivery time slot.
✓ Deployed and accessible via a public URL such as Vercel, Netlify, or Railway. The demo must load without errors and environment variables must be secured.
Bonus (extra points)

⭐ MoMo payment: MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money checkout flow. Real MTN MoMo API integration earns full marks.
⭐ 3 languages: English, French, Kinyarwanda. Full UI translation with a visible header switcher; Kinyarwanda must cover all core strings.
⭐ Product detail page: dedicated URL, full info, quantity selector, Add to Cart/Wishlist, related products carousel, and breadcrumb navigation.
⭐ Dark mode: light/dark toggle, preference saved to localStorage, and system preference respected on first load.
⭐ Great product images: real Cloudinary images from the dataset, Unsplash/Pexels fallbacks, no broken links, and lazy loading.
⭐ Order tracking: status timeline from Placed to Confirmed to Packed to Out for Delivery to Delivered, with past orders in account.
⭐ Wishlist / favourites: persists for logged-in users, with one-click move to cart.
⭐ Promotions & deals: dedicated page, discount badges, and countdown timer for limited offers.
⭐ Admin dashboard: password-protected, view orders, revenue, and top products; mark orders as confirmed or delivered.
⭐ Smart reorder: Buy Again section from past orders, with one-click re-add of a full previous order to cart.
⭐ Product reviews & ratings: 1-5 stars plus text review for purchased products; average rating shown on cards and detail pages.
⭐ Real-time stock indicator: In Stock, Low Stock, Out of Stock states; Notify Me waitlist for out-of-stock items.
⭐ Cart upsell: 3-5 complementary product suggestions that update dynamically as cart contents change.
⭐ Scheduled & recurring orders: weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly repeat at checkout; next delivery shown in dashboard.
⭐ Performance budget: Lighthouse mobile score >= 80, LCP <= 3.5s, CLS <= 0.1; WebP/AVIF images, no render-blocking scripts; submit Lighthouse screenshot.
Dataset

789 real products scraped from simbaonlineshopping.com across 10 categories — real product images hosted on Cloudinary, all prices in Rwandan Francs (RWF).

Download simba_products.json
